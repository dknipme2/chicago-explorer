## 11. QUIRKY / WEIRD CHICAGO

**International Museum of Surgical Science** (you already have this)
- 1524 N Lake Shore Dr, Chicago 60610 (Gold Coast)
- Lat/Lng: 41.9108, -87.6261
- What: North America's ONLY museum devoted to surgery. Housed in a century-old mansion modeled after Versailles. Life-sized models, unsettling wax figures, exhibits on anatomy, apothecaries, surgical technology, and the horrors of past medical practice.
- Price: $20 adults
- Route: Downtown lakefront

**Intuit Art Museum (Center for Intuitive and Outsider Art)**
- West Town neighborhood, Chicago
- Lat/Lng: ~41.8877, -87.6608
- What: One of only a few museums worldwide dedicated to self-taught and outsider art. Houses Henry Darger's apartment recreation — Darger was a reclusive janitor who created a 15,000-page fantasy epic with hundreds of watercolor paintings. Haunting and utterly unique.
- Price: Free-$15
- Route: Near West Loop — ON the route

**Leather Archives & Museum**
- Chicago (Rogers Park area)
- What: First collection in the country focused on leather/BDSM subculture history. Eight exhibition galleries + library + auditorium. Provocative and educational.
- Price: $10
- Route: DETOUR far north

**Weird Chicago Tours**
- Departs from 600 N Clark St, Chicago
- What: Bus tour of Chicago's most haunted, bizarre, and macabre sites. Ghost stories, crime scenes, oddities. Multiple tour options.
- Price: ~$43+
- Route: Downtown departure — ON the route

**Chicago Pedway Underground Tour**
- Loop area, Chicago
- What: 2-hour mostly indoor walking tour through Chicago's hidden underground passages — stained glass, artwork, underground swimming pool views. Quirky and off-the-beaten-path.
- Price: ~$25-35
- Route: ON the route — Loop

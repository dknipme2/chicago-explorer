This app was built just for your visit. We researched Bib Gourmand spots, hidden oddity shops, the best doughnuts in the city, baklava bakeries, exotic snack aisles, and way more — then organized everything into theme trails you can explore.

How it works
🥟 Pick a trail — like Dumpling Universe, Sweet Tooth, or Curiosity Cabinet
⭐ Every spot has a score and a "must try" item so you know what to order
📍 Sort by nearest to find what's close to you right now
🗺️ Map tab shows everything on a map with directions
🏠 Based from Glen Ellyn — spots on the route to the city are marked
